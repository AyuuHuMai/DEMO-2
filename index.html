Budget Setup

A budget setup featuring the NVIDIA GTX 1650 (4GB VRAM) is an entry-level gaming PC designed for 1080p gaming, esports, and productivity on a restricted budget. It is highly valued for its power efficiency—most models do not require a PCIe power connector, drawing power directly from the motherboard, making it perfect for upgrading older pre-built office PCs.

Mid-Range setup

A mid-range gaming setup centered around the NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 (typically the 12GB version) is considered a versatile, value-focused choice, offering strong performance for 1080p and entry-level 1440p gaming

High-End Setup

A high-end NVIDIA RTX 4090 setup is the premier consumer workstation and gaming configuration, designed for maximum performance, 4K/8K resolution gaming, and intensive creative or AI workloads. Released as the flagship of the Ada Lovelace architecture, it delivers exceptional ray-tracing capabilities, AI-driven DLSS 3 frame generation, and 24 GB of GDDR6X VRAM.
